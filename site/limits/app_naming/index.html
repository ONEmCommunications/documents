    App naming

    Application naming¶

    When registering your applications, make sure you keep in mind the following: - the name must be unique; this is enforced by the create application form - the name should have one word, or multiple without spaces - choose a significant and easy to remember name; keep in mind that users will use # followed by the name you choose to access your application

    For free plans, the name will be automatically assigned to your application.
